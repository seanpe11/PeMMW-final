About

This website was hastily put together in a few days to hopefully help compensate a student's failing grade. It follows a second-person (slightly) interactive story, is kinda cheesy, but hopefully well put together and enjoyable. The mathematical concepts for the story were topics taught in GE Math in the Modern World. Sources for topics:
