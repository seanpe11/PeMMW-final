"ANGSARAPNGJOLLIBEE". Yep, this was definitely your brother's password alright. Almost instantly, as soon as you press the last key to the password, the safe pops open with a much nicer beep this time. You look inside; there is money. As you begin counting the cash, you realize that the amount of money inside is actually quite impressive, for a lifestyle of gambling. However, despite it being a considerable amount, you realize with despair that as the uncounted money begins to dwindle that this will not be not nearly enough. You look closer into the safe. At the bottom of the pile where the money was, you notice yet another sticky note that you did not notice the first time in your rush to count the money.

You sigh. Your brother wasn't that bad a person, and definitely a good writer (with quite a flair for the dramatic), but he most definitely made the poorest life decisions. Even though the business of bailing him out generally falls onto you (most especially in this case), you can't help it. He's your brother.

With a sudden jolt, however, you realize that you haven't even called the police yet! Well, that's fine. Now, when you do call them, you can give them this information. It is quite complex, but you realize that you could probably figure out who did it once you analyze the statements your brother gave.
